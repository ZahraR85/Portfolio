Wedding designer

Technologies used: JavaScript, React, MongoDB, TailwindCSS, Html, Css.

See Project with Render's Site See Project in Github

Homepage: Visitors can explore the Homepage to view an overview of services, a gallery, information about us, the menu, and additional pages via the footer. Registered users gain access to exclusive services, including sending guest invitations via email, booking a venue for their wedding date, and selecting catering, photography, music, and makeup services. To access these features, users must first sign in.

Register Form: Users can create an account to access personalized wedding services.

Sign-In Form: Registered users can log in to manage their wedding plans.

Admins have exclusive access to an advanced Admin Panel for managing various features. They can handle gallery images with pagination, manage venues with multiple images and Google Maps integration, Manage music bands and musicians, and organize catering categories like starters, main courses, drinks, and desserts, with options to add, update, or delete entries. This pages just can access with Admin's user and if you normally registered in Website cannot access them and see these part in Navbar.

invitation of Guests by Email: user can send an invitation Card by Email for all of their guests and they can see the invitation in their email and RSVP for the wedding. Also manage their answers here.

Search Venue by City, Price, Capacity:by click in each cards can see multiple image of each venue in detail and also can book it which in next slide can see the booking page.

Booking Venue:Users must enter their wedding date on the "User Information Page" before booking a venue and will be redirected there if it's not provided. If the selected venue is already booked for the chosen date, the system will display a conflict message and user must choose another venue. Otherwise, booking will be done and add to Shopping Cart.

Catering: Users can select favorite food by separating category of "starter", "main-Course", "Dessert", "Cold drink", "cake and Sweets", ... and by click on the "add to List" , he will see them in table in above of the page and can edit quantity and add details for each food he need. if he press the button of "Add to shopping Cart" the Grand price can add in his shopping card or update it. User can select photography, Music and Makeup Items from other page like this and update or delete them and add in shopping card.

dashboard: Users can track a countdown to their wedding day, manage a to-do list, and view all selected services like photography, catering, and even the name and image of venue which they selected in here and manage his choices.

My website is fully responsive, ensuring seamless adaptation to mobile devices. I’ve implemented a mobile-friendly navbar design to enhance usability, providing an optimized experience across all pages for mobile users.

Shopping Card: Users can review and manage their selected services with prices, or remove items before going to checkout. I used Stripe Site for checkout process here!
